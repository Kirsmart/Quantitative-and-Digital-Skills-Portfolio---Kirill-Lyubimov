In this project I conducted a customer base audit for KazMarket, an online company, to evaluate the health and the structure of company's customer base.
This analysis developed my ability to apply customer analytics to real business data from KazMarket. Using decile analysis, I have segmented 31,797 customers by annual spend and computed some concentration metrics, like AOF, AOV, and margin by tier to quantify differences in customer value. Through single and multi-cohort analysis, I have indicated revenue per active customer, and order frequency over time, identifying early patterns and measuring how cohort quality evolved across purchase periods. An annual performance table then allowed me to synthesise cross-cohort revenue contributions and assess the overall health of the customer base.
